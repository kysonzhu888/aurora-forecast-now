About Aurora Forecast Now

Aurora Forecast Now is a city-level northern lights forecast built from public space weather data and local cloud forecasts. It is designed for quick viewing decisions, not scientific guarantees.

Forecasts can change quickly. Always check local weather and safety conditions before driving to a dark-sky site.
